Our Public Market Strategies

Two Distinct Areas
of Specialized Expertise

Strategy One

Biotechnology Equities

HighVista has built deep, dedicated expertise in biotechnology equity investing — a sector where the intersection of scientific complexity, regulatory risk, and binary clinical outcomes creates persistent information asymmetry and a durable edge for specialists.

Our biotechnology team combines rigorous scientific analysis with financial expertise to identify companies where the market misprices the probability and magnitude of clinical success. This disciplined approach targets asymmetric risk/reward profiles: limited downside in companies with strong pipelines and balance sheets, and significant upside from catalytic events.

  • Deep scientific due diligence on clinical pipeline and mechanism of action
  • Focus on information asymmetry and market mispricing of binary events
  • Portfolio construction designed for asymmetric return profiles
  • Active management through catalyst-driven events and binary readouts
Strategy Two

Hedged Public Market Strategy

HighVista's Hedged Public Market Strategy is designed to achieve strong risk-adjusted returns while maintaining deliberately low net exposure to the global equity market — providing a genuine alternative return stream for investors seeking to reduce their dependence on equity market beta.

The strategy combines long positions in companies with differentiated, underappreciated business models with short positions in overvalued, deteriorating businesses — a portfolio designed to generate alpha on both sides while managing overall market exposure with discipline.

  • Low net equity market exposure — an authentic alternative to long-only equity
  • Long/short construction targeting differentiated alpha generation on both sides
  • Rigorous risk management including factor exposure, position sizing, and sector limits
  • Designed to complement private market allocations within a broader alternatives portfolio
The HighVista Edge in Public Markets

Why HighVista in
Public Markets?

Sector Specialization

Generalist public equity managers cannot compete with specialists in complex, knowledge-intensive sectors like biotechnology. HighVista's dedicated team has the scientific and financial expertise to identify opportunities that generalists miss.

True Market Neutrality

Many "alternative" public equity strategies are simply levered beta. HighVista's hedged strategy is built from the ground up to maintain genuinely low market exposure, providing authentic diversification for client portfolios.

Integration with Private Markets

HighVista's public markets strategies benefit from — and contribute to — the firm's broad alternative investment platform. Insights from private equity and venture capital inform public market views, and vice versa.

Platform Integration

Public & Private: A
Unified Investment Platform

HighVista is one of a small number of alternative asset managers that maintains genuine expertise in both private and public markets — and deliberately integrates insights across the two.

Our biotechnology equity team shares research and analysis with the venture capital team's life sciences investments. Our hedged public market team provides real-time market intelligence to the broader investment platform. The multi-asset strategy benefits from the full breadth of both capabilities.

This integration creates a compounding informational advantage: insights generated in private markets inform public market positioning, and the real-time signals from public markets improve private investment decision-making. It is a genuine platform effect that single-strategy managers cannot replicate.
