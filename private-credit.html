Private Markets

Private Credit

HighVista's private credit strategy targets specialized lending and opportunistic credit opportunities in undercapitalized markets — generating equity-like returns through disciplined underwriting and structural expertise.

Strategy Overview

Beyond Traditional
Direct Lending

HighVista's private credit strategy is built on a simple but powerful observation: the most attractive risk-adjusted returns in credit markets are found not in the crowded direct lending market, but in more specialized, off-the-run segments that are structurally undercapitalized and harder to access.

Our approach focuses on opportunistic credit opportunities — those that require more work to source, greater expertise to underwrite, and deeper relationships to access. These segments are systematically underserved by traditional credit providers, creating a persistent premium for patient, skilled capital.

The strategy takes a multi-strategy approach, investing across real estate-backed credits, corporate credits, and uncorrelated credit opportunities. Each investment must offer compelling risk-adjusted returns that cannot be replicated through traditional direct lending.

Core Principle

Outperform Traditional Direct Lending

By avoiding crowded direct lending and focusing on more specialized, esoteric credit, we seek higher risk-adjusted returns than the traditional private credit market can offer.

Approach

Opportunistic & Flexible

Our credit portfolio spans special situations, secondaries, junior capital, esoteric assets, and other segments that offer equity-like return profiles within a credit structure.

Edge

Proprietary Sourcing & Underwriting

Success in off-the-run credit requires proprietary deal sourcing, sophisticated structuring, and the ability to underwrite transactions that generalist lenders cannot or will not.

Investment Segments

A Multi-Strategy
Credit Approach

HighVista's private credit strategy invests across three core segments, each chosen for its structural inefficiency and return premium over traditional lending.

Real Estate-Backed Credits

Loans and credit facilities secured by real estate assets — including transitional properties, special situations, and complex collateral structures — that fall outside the appetite of traditional real estate lenders.

Corporate Credits

Specialized corporate lending including special situations, rescue financing, complex capital structures, and junior debt in the lower middle market — segments where HighVista's private equity expertise creates a meaningful underwriting edge.

Uncorrelated Credit Opportunities

Esoteric credit assets — structured products, royalty streams, litigation finance, and other specialized instruments — that offer attractive returns with low correlation to traditional financial markets.
