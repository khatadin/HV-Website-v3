HighVista has been investing in the lower middle market for over 20 years — targeting small, privately held U.S. businesses where less competition and structural inefficiency create durable return premiums.

Strategy Overview

The Case for the
Lower Middle Market

The U.S. lower middle market represents one of the most structurally attractive segments in private equity. These are businesses generally with enterprise values below $150 million — too small for large buyout firms, too complex for generalist investors, and therefore systematically underserved by capital.

In this segment, HighVista observes less competition as a provider of capital, more attractive entry valuations, and greater opportunity to add value through operational and strategic improvements. The combination of lower purchase prices, less leverage, and more room for value creation consistently produces better risk-adjusted returns than large-cap buyout.

HighVista's private equity team has been investing in the lower middle market since the firm's founding in 2004 — more than two decades of continuously refined process, network, and market knowledge. This experience creates a compounding advantage that is difficult to replicate.

Lower middle market portfolio companies can be sold to strategic buyers, financial sponsors, or taken public — a broader exit universe that improves liquidity optionality and competitive dynamics at exit.

Investment Approach

A Deliberate Mix of
Investment Structures

HighVista's lower middle market funds invest through a carefully balanced combination of three complementary investment structures, each offering distinct return and risk characteristics.

Primary

Primary Fund Commitments

Commitments to sub-$500 million specialist private equity sponsors — established, sector-focused managers with proven track records in the lower middle market who provide diversified exposure to the segment.

Co-Investment

Direct Co-Investments

Direct equity investments alongside funded and independent sponsors, providing concentrated exposure to specific companies at reduced fees and with the benefit of HighVista's proprietary underwriting and due diligence.

Secondary

Lower Middle Market Secondaries

Selective purchases of lower middle market secondary interests at discounts to net asset value — providing diversified, de-risked exposure with near-term cash flow and accelerated return of capital.
