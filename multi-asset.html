Multi-Asset
Alternatives

HighVista's flagship strategy — a carefully diversified, risk-managed portfolio of alternative investments designed to serve as a reliable core for sophisticated investors seeking differentiated returns.

Strategy Overview

The Backbone of
HighVista's Platform

HighVista began investing on behalf of clients in 2005 with a commingled multi-asset class, multi-alternatives offering. More than two decades later, this strategy remains the cornerstone of our investment platform — the fullest expression of our philosophy and capabilities in a single portfolio.

The Multi-Asset Alternative Strategy is designed to be an all-weather core investment that provides clients with broad exposure to HighVista's entire suite of private and public market capabilities. The portfolio is built to deliver strong risk-adjusted returns across market environments, with thoughtful diversification across asset classes, geographies, and return drivers.

Unlike a traditional fund-of-funds, HighVista manages this strategy with complete integration — there is no division between the managers of underlying strategies and the architects of the overall portfolio. The same investment team that makes individual strategy decisions also constructs and manages the multi-asset portfolio.

Investment Philosophy

Investment Philosophy

HighVista's philosophy for generating enduring alpha rests on two interconnected pillars: where and how to seek excess returns, and how to construct a portfolio that captures them durably across market cycles.

HighVista's Approach to Alpha

Less Competitive Markets with Tailwinds

Seek areas with less competition and efficiency to generate excess returns; ideally with thematic tailwinds.

Partner with Specialists with Edge

Specialists may benefit from better sourcing, underwriting, structuring, and asset management.

Flexible Structure & Implementation

Active user of custom structures, co-investments, and direct investments to obtain access to attractive deals well beyond fund investments.

HighVista's Approach to Portfolio Construction

Downside Protection & Tail Risk Management

Protect against significant portfolio losses — these are very difficult to recoup.

True Diversification & Macro-Light Exposures

Seek out drivers of return that fundamentally are less correlated with the broad economy.

Liquidity Management & Catalyst-Driven Emphasis

Catalyst-rich opportunities enable "bond-like" laddering and enhanced liquidity as well as diversification.

Tested Resilience

Proven Through Major
Market Stress Events

HighVista's investment team and risk management process have been tested through three distinct and severe market dislocations — with the Multi-Asset strategy navigating each without compromising its long-term objectives.

2008–2009

Global Financial Crisis

During the most severe market dislocation since the Great Depression, HighVista's emphasis on diversification, tail risk mitigation, and uncorrelated return sources demonstrated its value. The firm identified opportunistic entry points across credit and private markets during the dislocation.

2020

COVID-19 Pandemic

The rapid market selloff and equally rapid recovery of 2020 tested portfolio liquidity management and the resilience of the private markets book. The strategy's broad diversification and active risk management helped navigate extreme volatility while preserving capital for opportunistic deployment.

2022

Rate Shock

The most rapid rate hiking cycle in decades created significant dislocations in public and private markets. HighVista's low correlation to traditional fixed income and its focus on structurally uncorrelated return sources provided meaningful downside protection during this environment.
