Our Story

Built on Conviction,
Driven by Curiosity

HighVista Strategies is an employee-owned alternative asset manager with a singular mission: to help sophisticated investors achieve enduring, outsized alpha in structurally inefficient markets.

Who We Are

Twenty Years of Disciplined
Alternative Investing

Founded in Boston in 2004, HighVista Strategies was built on a simple but powerful thesis: the most durable investment alpha is found in corners of the market where structural inefficiencies persist — areas that are underfollowed, undercapitalized, or simply harder to access than the mainstream.

We began investing on behalf of clients in 2005 with a commingled multi-asset class, multi-alternatives offering. That multi-asset capability remains the backbone of HighVista's investment program today, and we have continuously expanded our platform to offer specialized strategies in both private and public markets.

In 2023, HighVista acquired the abrdn U.S. private markets business, significantly expanding our private equity, private credit, and venture capital capabilities. Today, we manage over $11.5 billion on behalf of clients globally, with a senior leadership group of twelve and a culture built on intellectual rigor and long-term alpha generation.

As a 100% employee-owned firm, every member of our team has a direct stake in delivering outstanding outcomes for our clients. Our interests are genuinely aligned — we invest our own capital alongside the investors we serve.

Our History

Two Decades of Growth

2004

Founded in Boston

André Perold and Daniel Jick co-found HighVista Strategies with a vision to build a disciplined, research-driven alternative asset manager focused on structurally inefficient markets.

2005

First Client Investments

HighVista begins investing on behalf of clients through its flagship multi-asset class, multi-alternatives offering — a strategy that continues to serve as the firm's investment backbone.

2010s

Platform Expansion

HighVista expands its private equity franchise to over a decade of consistent performance in the lower middle market, launching multiple oversubscribed fund vintages and building a reputation for access to hard-to-reach opportunities.

2022

Private Credit Fund II at $450M

HighVista closes its second opportunistic private credit fund at $450 million, reinforcing its commitment to specialized lending and off-the-run credit opportunities in undercapitalized market segments.

2023

Acquisition of abrdn U.S. Private Markets Business

HighVista acquires the abrdn U.S. private markets business, significantly expanding its private equity, private credit, and early-stage venture capital teams and capabilities.

2024

20 Years of Pursuing Alpha & Fund X Close

HighVista celebrates its 20th anniversary and closes Private Equity Fund X at $675 million, oversubscribed, demonstrating continued strong investor demand for the firm's lower middle market approach.

2025

$11.5B AUM & Fund XI at $800M

HighVista surpasses $11.5 billion in assets under management. Private Equity Fund XI closes oversubscribed at $800 million, and the venture capital team raises $270 million for its latest fund.

Our Competitive Advantage

The HighVista Edge

HighVista's approach to generating alpha is built on four enduring strengths that create a durable competitive advantage across market cycles.

01

Alpha Driven Focus

We are opportunistic and focused on finding less competitive and less efficient areas of the markets, which we view to be structurally attractive "ponds" to fish in. These areas require specialized skillsets to access and diligence.

02

Experienced and Networked

We have a long history of putting capital to work across a wide range of alternative investment opportunities. Over decades we have built a valuable network of partners, peer investors and other market participants, which significantly aid in our ability to uncover, source, and evaluate investments with a relative value mindset.

03

Flexible Structuring and Superior Execution

We seek to find the best way to build robust portfolios with flexibility to invest via joint ventures, co-investments, secondaries, special-purpose vehicles, primary funds and other bespoke arrangements. Our broad platform capability enables us to structure investment opportunities in a highly flexible and accretive fashion.

04

Alignment

We build strong alignment with our clients and investment partners alike. With meaningful personal investment alongside our clients, we foster a shared commitment to long-term success and a durable partnership with our investors.

Our Culture

The Values That Define Us

Our culture is not a set of platitudes — it is the operating system that drives how we think, invest, and serve our clients every day.

Long-Term Orientation

We invest with a long time horizon, resisting short-term pressures in favor of enduring alpha. Our employee-ownership model ensures our incentives are calibrated to the same horizon as our clients.

Intellectual Honesty

We encourage dissent and debate. Every investment thesis must withstand rigorous challenge, and we reward the courage to hold differentiated views even when they run counter to consensus.

Client Alignment

We co-invest alongside our clients in every strategy we offer. Our success is inseparable from theirs — a principle that shapes every decision we make from portfolio construction to risk management.

Responsibility

How We Operate

Generating alpha for our clients is our singular investment purpose — and we recognize that achieving it responsibly requires a commitment to principled decision-making, an inclusive team, and active engagement in the communities where we work and live.

Our Investing

HighVista's sole focus is generating attractive, sustainable alpha for our clients over the long term. As a steward of their capital, we recognize that every investment decision carries the potential to create or diminish that alpha. This understanding drives our commitment to thoughtful decision-making, principled investment practices, and careful consideration of material risks in any investment we may make.

Our Team

We are committed to building and nurturing an inclusive workplace with a team that brings diversity of backgrounds and experiences. We invest in our people — our greatest asset — and believe that a breadth of perspectives and inputs leads to better alpha generation and better outcomes for our clients and the firm.

Our Community

Our responsibility as a firm extends beyond the portfolio to the communities in which we operate. We encourage team members to support local charitable organizations through philanthropy and active engagement, and we cultivate opportunities for the team to volunteer together throughout the year. Many colleagues serve on Boards and Investment Committees where they provide governance and investment guidance for non-profit organizations doing important work in our communities.
