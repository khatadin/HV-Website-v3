Investment Access

How Clients Invest
with HighVista

HighVista offers clients flexibility in how they access our investment strategies. Investors may choose to participate in our commingled fund offerings for diversified alternatives exposure, or they may elect to invest in individual strategies to target specific segments of the market.

For institutional clients with specific investment policy guidelines, portfolio constraints, or scale requirements, HighVista also offers customized separate account mandates that can be tailored to meet individual needs.

Our client relations team works closely with investors and their advisors to ensure the right fit between client objectives and HighVista's available strategies, and to facilitate the ongoing management of the investor relationship with full transparency.

Commingled

Multi-Asset Alternative Strategy

A diversified portfolio spanning the full breadth of HighVista's capabilities — ideal for investors seeking a single alternatives allocation that provides broad exposure to private and public market opportunities.

Specialized

Single Strategy Access

Investors may also access individual HighVista strategies — private equity, private credit, venture capital, or public market strategies — to complement or enhance their existing portfolio allocations.

Custom

Separate Account Mandates

For institutional investors with specific requirements, HighVista structures customized mandates with tailored guidelines, reporting, and portfolio construction aligned to each client's unique investment objectives.

Why HighVista

What Our Clients Value Most

Our clients consistently highlight these qualities as defining characteristics of the HighVista partnership.

Genuine Alignment

HighVista invests its own capital alongside every client strategy. This co-investment model ensures that the firm's interests are always fully aligned with investor outcomes — not just in theory, but in practice.

Access to Inefficient Markets
